The following placeholders are available inside plugin configuration files only.

:::note
Looking for global placeholders? Check out the [PlaceholderAPI](papi.md) integration.
:::

### Currency

* `%currency_id%` - [ID](../features/currencies.md#currency-id) of the currency.
* `%currency_name%` - Currency's `Name` value.
* `%currency_symbol%` - Currency's `Symbol` value.
* `%currency_prefix%` - Currency's `Prefix` value.
* `%currency_label%` - First currency's command alias.
